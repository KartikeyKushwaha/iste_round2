An online cryptic hunt is a virtual event or game where participants are challenged to solve a series of cryptic puzzles, clues, or riddles. It typically takes place on the internet, utilizing websites, forums, social media platforms, or dedicated online platforms. Participants are usually given a set of clues or puzzles that they need to decipher in order to progress through the hunt. These clues can be in various forms, such as wordplay, hidden messages, ciphers, anagrams, or visual puzzles. The difficulty level can vary, ranging from relatively simple to highly complex challenges. Online cryptic hunts often have a specific theme or storyline that ties the puzzles together, creating an immersive and engaging experience for the participants. They can be organized by puzzle enthusiasts, gaming communities, educational institutions, or event organizers. Participants typically compete against each other or work together in teams to solve the puzzles and progress through the hunt. The first person or team to complete all the puzzles or reach the final solution is usually declared the winner or receives some form of recognition or reward.

What is ISTE?

With more than 1500 student chapters and more than 6 lakh devoted members worldwide, ISTE is one of the top nonprofit organizations in our country. It has a solid reputation for offering technical education in a variety of institutions. For three years running, ISTE SC MANIT has been recognized as the best chapter in Central India.
